SERA REWORK — INTERACTIVE KIT DEMO
WHAT IS THIS?
This is an interactive demo for a proposed Seraphine mid lane kit rework by JLTHAKAD. It lets you play with the reworked abilities, see how they work in action, and experience the dual mana/energy resource system that makes this kit unique.
Choose a mode from the main menu to get started. Use the ⚙ Options button on the main menu (or the gear icon during gameplay) to configure hotkeys, items, boots, and stat values at any time — changes apply to all modes.
CONTROLS — MOVEMENT & ABILITIES
Right-Click
Move Seraphine to that position. Also used to directly target an enemy (right-click on them).
Hover
Aim — all abilities fire toward your cursor position.
Q
Listen (Q1) — Wide area burst. Costs Mana. Deals max HP% damage and applies Soul Read (reveals enemy cooldowns).
During the recast window, press Q again for Speak (Q2) — Precision burst. Costs Energy. Deals missing HP% damage and applies Tempo Break (reduces enemy ability haste).
W
Set the Stage — Place a guard stage at your feet (up to 3 active). Guards absorb damage while you stand on them. When a guard breaks, you gain +1 Energy. Free cost — no mana or energy required.
E
Piercing Cry — Line skillshot. Costs Mana. Deals magic damage and shatters enemy shields on contact.
R
Cover Your Ears — Massive soundwave. Costs Energy ×2. Silences enemies and reduces healing. Deals no damage itself, but enemies hit take amplified damage from Q1 and Q2. If an enemy dies to Q2 while silenced, one guard cooldown resets.
⚙ Tip
Enable Quick Cast with Indicator in Controls settings to see ability range/area when holding a key. Release the key to cast, or right-click / Escape to cancel — just like League of Legends.
CONTROLS — AUTO-ATTACK & ORB WALKING
Auto-attack controls work in Sandbox mode and can be configured in ⚙ Options → Controls.
A
Attack Move — Shows attack range indicator. Left-click to confirm attack on nearest enemy to your cursor (or nearest to Sera, depending on setting).
2
Attack Move Click — Instant attack-move with no confirmation needed. Great for kiting: right-click away → press 2 → right-click away → repeat.
X
Stop / Hold Position — Cancels all movement and auto-attacks. Sera stands still.
`
Target Champions Only — Toggle. When active, auto-attacks and attack-move ignore non-champion targets.
Orb Walking: After your auto-attack projectile fires, you can immediately right-click to move during the recovery animation, then attack-move again. This lets you weave movement between attacks for better kiting and chasing — a core League mechanic.
DUAL RESOURCE SYSTEM
Seraphine uses Mana and Energy simultaneously — the first champion in League to do so.
Mana — Regenerates naturally over time. Fuels Q1 (Listen) and E (Piercing Cry). These abilities are always available for poking and trading in lane.
Energy — Does NOT regenerate passively. Only gained when a guard breaks on your W stage. Fuels Q2 (Speak) and R (Cover Your Ears). You must absorb damage on your stages to unlock your strongest abilities.
The core loop: place stages → absorb damage → guards break → energy fills → unleash devastating combos.
PASSIVE — DAMPENER / AMPLIFIER
Seraphine's passive changes based on whether she is standing on a W stage or not.
Off Stage — Dampener Mode
Reduced ability range. Passively weakens nearby enemy ability effectiveness. This is your default state — safe but limited.
On Stage — Amplifier Mode
Increased ability range. Your next basic ability echoes — it casts a second time automatically at reduced power. This is your power state — commit to a stage to unlock your full potential.
MODE — PRACTICE
Fight against an AI dummy that uses abilities and attacks you in a mid lane environment. A jungler will periodically gank from the sides.
How to Play
Right-click to move, hover to aim, use Q/W/E/R to fight the dummy. Place W stages to absorb damage and generate energy. Try to kill the dummy using full combos. The dummy respawns after death so you can practice indefinitely.
Turrets
Your ally turret (bottom of lane) and enemy turret (top of lane) are both active. Standing in enemy turret range will get you shot — turrets have dashed range circles so you can see the danger zone.
Jungler Ganks
An enemy jungler appears from the left or right side periodically. Survive ganks by retreating through your W stages — each guard absorbs damage as you run. The jungler will leave after ~12 seconds.
MODE — WATCH DEMO
This mode lets you fight freely like Practice mode, but also includes a full AI demo. Hit the ▶ Watch Full Demo button to let the AI take over and perform 6 scripted combo demonstrations.
Navigation
Use the ◀ PREV and NEXT ▶ buttons at the top to skip between rounds. Each round showcases a specific mechanic:
Round 1: Lane Trade — stages → absorb → R silence → Q burst
Round 2: R+Q2 Guard Reset — kill with Q2 while silenced
Round 3: Shield Breaker — E shatters shield → burst
Round 4: All-In Execute — full energy instant combo
Round 5: Extended Fight — tanky target, 2 full rotations
Round 6: Energy Refund on Death — die with guards up → energy refund → respawn execute
What to Watch
Pay attention to the ability panel on the right — it highlights which ability is being used. The Q slot visually swaps between Listen (Q1) and Speak (Q2) during recast windows. Watch how energy fills as guards break, and how the full combo chains together.
MODE — AIM TRAINER
A 10-level dodge-and-aim challenge. Colored targets spawn across the arena — match the correct ability to each target color while dodging incoming red projectiles.
● PINK
Press Q to hit. Worth 100 pts.
● GOLD ★
Press Q — smaller target, worth 200 pts (2× bonus for precision).
● CYAN
Press E first to shatter the shield, THEN Q to finish. Worth 250 pts total.
● PURPLE
Press R first to silence, THEN Q for amplified damage. Worth 400 pts total.
● RED
DODGE these projectiles! Getting hit off stage costs -50 pts and resets your streak. Standing on a W stage blocks the damage.
No Cooldowns
Abilities have zero cooldown and zero cost in this mode. Focus purely on aiming and dodging.
Scoring
Hits build a streak multiplier (+10% per consecutive hit, max 3×). Misses cost -30 pts. Targets that expire without being hit reset your streak. Levels are graded from S+ through F.
Difficulty
Level 1 is slow with big targets and light incoming fire. Level 10 has tiny, fast-moving targets with relentless projectile storms. Each level lasts 60–120 seconds.
MODE — SANDBOX CONFIGURATOR
A full stat-modding lab where you can configure every number in Seraphine's kit before launching.
What You Can Configure
Base stats (HP, Mana, Armor, MR, AD, AP, Ability Haste), all ability values (damage, scaling, cooldowns, radii, speeds), passive settings (Dampener reduction, Amplifier range, Echo damage), 6 custom item slots with stat components and unique passives/actives, boots, and auto-attack parameters.
S
Spawn a configured dummy at your cursor (uses your Dummy tab settings for HP, damage, abilities, and behavior).
D
Spawn a chaser dummy that actively pursues Seraphine.
H
Spawn a healer pair — two dummies that heal each other.
TAB
Toggle the stats overlay — shows your active configuration values on screen.
⚙ CONFIG
Button in the top-left returns to the full sandbox configurator at any time.
Dummies
Dummies have full ability kits (Q/W/E/R) with configurable damage, cooldowns, shields, and AI behavior. They can be set to chase, pace, or stand still.
Items
Items provide AP, HP, Mana, Armor, MR, Ability Haste, Magic Pen, and unique passives like Resonance, Stasis Field, and Execute. Configure them in ⚙ Options → Items.
Auto-Attacks
Sandbox includes the full auto-attack engine with orb walking, attack-move, and stop commands. Configure hotkeys and AA stats in ⚙ Options → Controls.
ABOUT THE REWORK
Every ability in this kit is pulled directly from Seraphine's biography and color story.
The dual resource system reflects her journey — mana is her natural gift (always available), energy is power earned through suffering (guards must break).
The Dampener/Amplifier passive mirrors her transformation from repressed child to performing artist. Her W stages represent the guards she watched people refuse to put down.

Dual Resource System
Mana (left bar) fuels Q1 and E — always available. Energy (right bar, 3 segments) fuels Q2 and R — only gained when guards fall. She must endure damage to unlock her strongest abilities.
P
Dampener / Amplifier
Off stage: Dampener mode — reduced range, passively reduces enemy ability effectiveness nearby. On stage: Amplifier mode — increased range, next basic ability echoes (casts twice).
Her parents crafted a dampener to repress her powers. She dismantled it and gave the crystal a new home — a platform that amplifies her gifts.
Q
Listen / Speak
Q1: MANA · Q2: ENERGY
Q1 — Listen MANA: Wide area burst dealing max HP damage. Applies Soul Read — reveals all enemy ability cooldowns. Easy to land for scouting and poking.
Q2 — Speak ENERGY: Pinpoint area dealing missing HP burst. Applies Tempo Break — reduces enemy ability haste. Demands precision aim. Enemies under R take amplified damage.
"She heard every person's soul, loving or cruel." Q1 reads them. Q2 disrupts them. Listen first, endure, then speak.
W
Set the Stage
FREE
NO COST — Press up to 3 times. Each places a stage with its own guard. Guards absorb damage while Sera stands on the stage. When a guard falls: +1 Energy. Stages persist until guard breaks. If Sera dies, all remaining guards break and grant energy for respawn.
"Why can't they put their guards down for one moment?" Guards must come down for something real to happen — and when they do, she gains her power.
E
Piercing Cry
MANA
MANA — Soundwave in a line dealing magic damage and shattering enemy shields. Always available as part of her proactive trading kit alongside Q1.
Her arc is about dismantling barriers — the dampener, the divide between cities. An ability that strips shields fits breaking through so people can truly be heard.
R
Cover Your Ears
ENERGY ×2
ENERGY — Massive soundwave that silences enemies and reduces healing. Deals no damage. Resets travel distance on champion hit. Enemies affected take increased damage from Q1 and Q2. If enemy dies to Q2 while silenced, one guard cooldown refreshes.
She hid shivering, hands over her ears, unable to hear herself above the chaos. Now her music is so powerful it forces enemies to cover theirs.
